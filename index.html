About Us

Hello, my name is Liene, I am 18 years old and I am in my last year of multimedia. I like to work with cars, mopeds, photography etc...
It seemed great to me to be able to put everything I like to do on one site, Fancar. You will find all kinds of photos, videos, ...

At the bottom of this page you can contact us for possible cooperation whether this is for a moped or car, both are possible.
